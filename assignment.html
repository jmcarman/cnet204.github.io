Assignment - Fall 2022 - Due Friday, November 4th at 11:59pm

Overview

"The power of the Web is in its universality. Access by everyone regardless of disability is an essential aspect." - Tim Berners-Lee, W3C Director and inventer of the World Wide Web. To accomplish this, the W3C has outlined accessibility standards as recommended best practices to follow.

You're going to research the W3C Web Accessibility Initiative(WAI) and create a webpage (detailed below).

Specifications

  1. Create a new webpage called assignment.html
  2. Add a link to your new page in your website's navigation bar (on all affected pages - index.html, lab5.html). (1 mark)
  3. Add a comment with your name and student number. (1 mark)
  4. Add your updated navigation bar with your active class on the appropriate option. (2 marks)
  5. You should use the same stylesheet you've created in the labs.
  6. Implement any two of the WAI standards (that haven't already been met by following the lab instructions) into your website. (10 marks)
  7. On the new page, include a 250-500-word explaination about why the WAI standards are important and discuss the examples you used. (6 marks)

Submission

Your assignment should be uploaded to the web server and the dropbox as a zip file by the due date. A late penalty of 10% per day will apply. If you require assistance zipping your files for submission, please ask in class.
